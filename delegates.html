What to expect at
Day of Code


Day of Code is an inspirational and hands-on day. By the end you will have built a working website, heard from inspirational people at the intersection of publishing and technology, and be provided with the (free) tools and inspiration to carry on coding. Here's what the day looks like:

09.00 - Introductory talks from our key sponsors, Hachette, FutureBook and Consonance, at Hachette's building on the Embankment in London

09.15 - Get into groups

09.30 - Coaches take groups to pre-designated meeting rooms. Delegates start to build their own website on their own laptop

12.30 - Basement - lightning talks while lunching

13.30 - Back to meeting rooms to finish up the websites

16.30 - Basement - wrap up & goodbye

Finish by 17.00

All the materials you'll use on the day will be published on this website nearer the time, and available after the event, too.
